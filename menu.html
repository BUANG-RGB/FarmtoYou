Valencia City

A prominent city nestled in the heart of Bukidnon, is a place where the vibrant city unites with the breathtaking natural scenery. It is also known as the “City of Golden Harvest”, as it plays a vital roles in securing and nurturing the region’s agricultural production. The city has also preserved ou culture and the deeply rooted heritage, establishing a gateway to the region’s culture, where visitors can explore the historical landmarks and immerse with the lively festivals.
